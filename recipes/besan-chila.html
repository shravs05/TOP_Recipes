Besan Chila Recipe

Description

Besan Chila is an Indian dish prepared using gram flour or chickpea flour. It contains salt, green chilli , onion, garlic, coriander and other spices.

Ingredients

  • Gram flour
  • Salt
  • Green Chilli
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Coriander Leaves
  • Curd
  • Ghee or Butter
  • Spices like Sabji Masala (search on google, it is a blend of many spices in powdered form.)

Steps

  1. Take 2-3 teaspoons full of gram flour in an empty glass.
  2. Add some water and and 1 spoon curd to it, and stir the mixture so that it forms a kind of fluid paste.
  3. Cut onion, green chilli and garlic into small pieces.
  4. Add sabji masala, coriander leaves, onion, garlic to the mixture and stir for mixing.
  5. Now heat a non-stick frying pan.
  6. As soon as the pan heats up, add some ghee/butter on it.
  7. Now, pour the paste prepared in above steps on the pan and let it cook on sim for a few minutes.
  8. After 2-3 minutes flip the chila and cook the top side.
  9. Now, switch off the burner and serve the chila with tomato ketchup and chai.
  10. A delicious and quickly prepared dish is ready to satiate your hunger.
