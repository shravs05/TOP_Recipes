Tehri

Description

Tehri is a rice dish prepared with vegetables like onion, peas, tomato, potato, garlic, etc.

Ingredients

  • 1 small bowl of rice
  • Green Chilli
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ginger paste
  • Peas
  • Potato
  • Tomato
  • Capsicum
  • Carrot
  • Paneer
  • Mustard Oil
  • Spices like sabji masala or birayani masala
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Heat 2 teaspoon of mustard oil in a pressure cooker
  2. Add chopped onion, garlic and green chilli in the cooker when the oil is hot and let the mixture cook on sim.
  3. When the onion turns brownish, add other vegetables after chopping them to the mixture and let them cook.
  4. Now, add salt and spices as per taste and add chopped paneer. Now, cook the mixture for another 2 minutes on sim.
  5. Simultaneously, take the bowl of rice and wash it two three times with tap water and sift the rice from water.
  6. Pour the rice in the pressure cooker and add around 2 glasses of water so that the water level stays an inch above the mixture.
  7. Close the lid of pressure cooker and let it cook for 2-3 whistles.
  8. Switch off the burner, and serve the tehri hot. It tastes best with curd and green chutney.
