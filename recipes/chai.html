Chai

Description

Chai is an Indian beverage prepared with milk, water, tea leaves and other ingredients. It tastes best when it is hot.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Water
  • Tea Leaves
  • Ginger
  • Cardamom
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Mix half cup water and 2/3 cup milk.
  2. Heat the mixture to boiling point.
  3. Now, set the burner at sim.
  4. Add one spoon tea leaves.
  5. Add half spoon grated ginger
  6. Crush one cardamom and add to the boiling mixture.
  7. Let it be on sim for 2 minutes.
  8. Now, switch off the burner and sift the beverage in a cup.
  9. Add sugar as per your taste
  10. A hot drink is ready to wake up your senses.
